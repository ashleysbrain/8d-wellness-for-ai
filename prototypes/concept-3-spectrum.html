The Framework

Total wellness is 8 dimensions, all working together.

DΨ

Psychological

The mind's capacity for self-awareness, emotional regulation, and cognitive resilience. How you process, adapt, and grow through experience.

DPh

Physical

The body as a biological system. Nutrition, movement, sleep, recovery, and the measurable markers of physiological health.

DIn

Intellectual

The drive to learn, create, and solve. Cognitive engagement, critical thinking, curiosity, and the pursuit of knowledge that expands your capability.

DSo

Social

The quality and depth of human connection. Relationships, community, communication, and the social structures that support or undermine wellbeing.

DSp

Spiritual

Meaning, purpose, and connection to something larger. Not religion specifically, but the human need for coherence, values alignment, and existential grounding.

DVo

Vocational

Your relationship with work and contribution. Career satisfaction, skill development, professional growth, and the alignment between what you do and who you are.

DFi

Financial

Economic stability and agency. Not wealth accumulation, but financial literacy, security, planning, and the freedom that comes from economic self-determination.

DEn

Environmental

The spaces you inhabit. Physical surroundings, safety, access to nature, air quality, noise, light, and the built environment's impact on every other dimension.

Who We Are

We study the system, not the symptoms.

Divinity Science is an interdisciplinary research lab applying quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science to the architecture of human health.

We don't treat symptoms. We study the system. Human wellness isn't one thing. It's eight distinct dimensions, each measurable, each improvable, each connected to the others in ways that current science is only beginning to map.

Our research sits at the intersection of fields that rarely talk to each other. Quantum biology and behavioral economics. Neuroscience and environmental psychology. Consciousness studies and financial wellness.

Founded by Ashley Williams, Founder & Chief of Research, Divinity Science exists to build the scientific foundation that the next generation of wellness technology will stand on.

"The connections between these fields aren't metaphorical. They're measurable."

Qubit Health

Where research becomes technology.

Qubit Health is the technology development hub of Divinity Science. Named after the qubit in quantum computing, it's where research becomes technology.

While Divinity Science asks the questions, Qubit Health builds the answers. Every tool, platform, and product that emerges from DS research is engineered here, with the same rigor applied to the code as to the science behind it.

The pipeline is simple: Divinity Science researches. Qubit Health builds. Products ship.

QUBIT · Flagship Product

Total wellness, personally measured.

QUBIT is the first technology designed to measure, track, and improve all 8 dimensions of wellness in a single integrated system. The AI gets to know you personally, learns your patterns, and evolves to address your specific needs.
