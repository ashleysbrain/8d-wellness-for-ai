Divinity Science

The research institution that wellness should be built on.

Divinity Science is an interdisciplinary research lab and technology firm. Founded in 2023 by Ashley Williams, Founder and Chief of Research, we work at the intersection where quantum theory meets neuroscience meets behavioral science meets data science.

Our core contribution is the 8-Dimension Wellness Framework: a comprehensive, measurable model for total human health. Not a trend. Not a philosophy. A scientific framework with testable predictions and real-world applications across every dimension of what it means to be well.

"We don't study one piece of health. We study the whole system, and we measure all of it."

Qubit Health: Technology Hub

Where research becomes technology.

Qubit Health is the technology development arm of Divinity Science. Named after the quantum bit, the fundamental unit of quantum information, it translates validated research into software, platforms, and tools.

The relationship is clear: Divinity Science discovers. Qubit Health engineers. QUBIT delivers. Every product traces directly back to the research.
