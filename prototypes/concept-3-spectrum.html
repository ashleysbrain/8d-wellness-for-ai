Who We Are

An interdisciplinary research lab.

Divinity Science sits at the intersection of quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science. We've identified 8 distinct dimensions of human wellness, each with its own metrics, interventions, and scientific literature.

This isn't one-size-fits-all wellness. It's systematic, color-coded, and quantifiable. Each dimension has been mapped, and the connections between them are where the real science lives.

Founded by Ashley Williams, Founder & Chief of Research

We don't believe human health is one thing. We've proven it's eight. And the technology to measure, track, and improve all eight simultaneously doesn't exist yet.

So we're building it.

The Framework

Eight dimensions. Eight sciences.

Psychological

Emotional regulation, cognitive patterns, and mental resilience. The dimension everything else is built on.

Physical

Movement, nutrition, sleep, and biological systems. The body's operating parameters, measured and optimized.

Intellectual

Continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and cognitive expansion. The mind's capacity to grow.

Social

Meaningful relationships, community building, and interpersonal dynamics. Connection as a biological need.

Spiritual

Purpose, meaning, and alignment with personal values. The dimension that gives direction to all others.

Vocational

Professional fulfillment, skill development, and career satisfaction. Work as a health dimension.

Financial

Economic stability, financial literacy, and resource management. Security as a wellness prerequisite.

Environmental

Physical surroundings, ecological health, and living conditions. The context that shapes everything else.

Technology Hub

Named after the quantum bit: the fundamental unit of quantum computing. Because understanding human wellness requires multi-dimensional, non-binary thinking.
