Who We Are

Research that treats wellness as a science, not a trend.

Divinity Science is an interdisciplinary research lab and technology firm. We sit at the intersection of quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science. Our work isn't theoretical. It's operational, measurable, and designed to scale.

We've identified 8 distinct dimensions of human wellness. Not a metaphor. A framework, with testable predictions and quantifiable metrics across every dimension. Each one maps to specific biological, psychological, and environmental systems.

The goal is simple: build the science that makes total human wellness possible. Then build the technology that delivers it.

Founded by Ashley Williams, Founder & Chief of Research

"We're not building another wellness product. We're building the scientific foundation that every wellness product should be built on."
The Framework

Eight dimensions of human wellness.

Total wellness isn't one thing. It's eight systems, interconnected and measurable. Each dimension has its own science, its own metrics, its own interventions.

D_Ψ / 01
Psychological

Emotional regulation, cognitive patterns, and mental resilience. The foundation everything else is built on.

D_Ph / 02
Physical

Movement, nutrition, sleep, and biological systems. The body's operating parameters, optimized through data.

D_In / 03
Intellectual

Continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and cognitive growth. The mind's capacity to expand.

D_So / 04
Social

Meaningful relationships, community building, and interpersonal dynamics. Connection as a biological need.

D_Sp / 05
Spiritual

Purpose, meaning, and alignment with personal values. The dimension that gives direction to all others.

D_Vo / 06
Vocational

Professional fulfillment, skill development, and career satisfaction. Work as a dimension of health.

D_Fi / 07
Financial

Economic stability, financial literacy, and resource management. Security as a wellness prerequisite.

D_En / 08
Environmental

Physical surroundings, ecological health, and living conditions. The context that shapes every other dimension.

Qubit Health

Where research becomes technology.

Qubit Health is the technology development hub of Divinity Science. It's where peer-reviewed research gets translated into working systems, protocols, and products people can actually use.

Think of it as the engineering arm. Divinity Science produces the science. Qubit Health builds the technology. And QUBIT is the flagship product that brings it all together.

Named after the quantum bit, the fundamental unit of quantum computing, because we believe wellness requires the same kind of multi-dimensional thinking.
