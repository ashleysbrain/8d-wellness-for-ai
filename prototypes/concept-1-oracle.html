Divinity Science

We don't study wellness trends. We build the science beneath them.

Divinity Science is an interdisciplinary research lab and technology firm. Founded in 2023 by Ashley Williams, we operate at the intersection of quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science.

Our work centers on the 8-Dimension Wellness Framework: a comprehensive, measurable model for understanding total human health. Not a checklist. Not a philosophy. A scientific framework with testable predictions and real-world applications.

Every technology we build, every partnership we form, every paper we publish flows from this core research. The framework isn't our product. It's the foundation everything else stands on.

Qubit Health: Technology Hub

Where research becomes technology.

Qubit Health is the technology development arm of Divinity Science. Named after the quantum bit, the fundamental unit of quantum information, it translates DS research into software, platforms, and tools.

Every product built by Qubit Health is grounded in peer-reviewed research from Divinity Science. The science comes first. The technology serves the science.

Our flagship product, QUBIT, is a comprehensive wellness intelligence platform launching in 2026. It tracks, analyzes, and supports all 8 dimensions of human health through personalized AI.
