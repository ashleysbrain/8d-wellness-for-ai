Who We Are

Research that changes what wellness means.

Divinity Science sits at the intersection of quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science. We're building the scientific foundation for understanding human health across all 8 dimensions, not just the ones that are easy to measure.

This isn't wellness as most people understand it. It's wellness as science: testable, measurable, reproducible. Every dimension maps to specific biological and environmental systems with quantifiable outcomes.

The work is interdisciplinary by necessity. Human health doesn't respect the boundaries between academic departments. Neither do we.

Founded by Ashley Williams, Founder & Chief of Research

"We're building the science that makes total human wellness possible. Then we're building the technology that delivers it."

Where Research Becomes Technology

The engineering arm of Divinity Science.

Qubit Health translates peer-reviewed research into working systems and products. It's where the 8-Dimensional Wellness Framework becomes software that people can actually use.

Named after the quantum bit, the fundamental unit of quantum computing. Because understanding human wellness requires the same kind of multi-dimensional, non-binary thinking that quantum systems demand.

QUBIT / Flagship Product

Your wellness. All 8 dimensions. One platform.

QUBIT is the first platform built on the 8-Dimensional Wellness Framework. Personalized AI that learns your patterns, adapts to your life, and works to improve your specific 8D profile over time.
