Who We Are

We build the science that wellness should be built on.

Divinity Science is an interdisciplinary research lab and technology firm. Founded in 2023 by Ashley Williams, Founder and Chief of Research, we operate where quantum theory meets neuroscience meets behavioral science meets data science.

Our core output is the 8-Dimension Wellness Framework: a measurable, testable model for understanding total human health. Not philosophy. Not opinion. Science with falsifiable predictions and cross-dimensional correlation data.

Everything we build flows from this research. The framework isn't what we sell. It's the reason everything we sell works.

"Wellness without measurement is just marketing. We measure."

The Framework

Wellness is 8 things working together.

Psychological
Mental health, emotional regulation, cognitive patterns, neurological resilience.
Physical
Physiological health, movement, nutrition, sleep architecture, biological markers.
Intellectual
Cognitive growth, learning capacity, creative problem-solving, knowledge systems.
Social
Interpersonal bonds, community integration, communication, relational health.
Spiritual
Purpose, meaning-making, existential grounding, consciousness exploration.
Vocational
Professional fulfillment, skill development, career alignment, productive contribution.
Financial
Economic stability, resource management, wealth-building behaviors, financial literacy.
Environmental
Physical surroundings, ecological connection, spatial design, environmental impact.

Qubit Health

Research becomes technology here.

Qubit Health is the technology arm of Divinity Science. Named after the quantum bit, the fundamental unit of quantum information, QH takes validated research and builds tools people can actually use.

The relationship is simple: Divinity Science discovers. Qubit Health engineers. QUBIT delivers. Every product traces back to published, testable research.

QUBIT: The Flagship Product

QUBIT is built by Qubit Health and grounded in Divinity Science research. It uses personalized AI to understand your patterns, track your progress, and help you improve across every dimension of health.
