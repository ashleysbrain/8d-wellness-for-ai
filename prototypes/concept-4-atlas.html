01 / Who We Are

We map the terrain that others haven't charted.

Divinity Science is an interdisciplinary research lab and technology firm. Founded in 2023 by Ashley Williams, Founder and Chief of Research, we operate at the intersection of quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science.

Our core contribution is the 8-Dimension Wellness Framework: a comprehensive, measurable model for total human health. A framework with testable predictions and real-world applications across every dimension of what it means to be well.

"Mapping the full terrain of human health. Not just the parts that are easy to measure."
Ashley Williams, Founder & Chief of Research

03 / Qubit Health

Where research becomes technology.

Qubit Health is the technology development arm of Divinity Science. Named after the quantum bit, the fundamental unit of quantum information, it translates validated research into software, platforms, and tools.

Divinity Science discovers. Qubit Health engineers. QUBIT delivers. Every product traces back to the research.
