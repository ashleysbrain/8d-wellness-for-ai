02 / Who We Are

Research as exploration.

Divinity Science is an interdisciplinary research lab and technology firm. We sit at the intersection of quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science, mapping territory that traditional wellness research hasn't charted.

We've identified 8 distinct dimensions of human wellness. Not a metaphor, not a marketing framework. A scientific map with testable coordinates, measurable distances, and quantifiable outcomes across every dimension.

The territory is vast. We're the ones drawing the maps.

Founded by Ashley Williams, Founder & Chief of Research

"We're not building another wellness product. We're mapping the entire landscape of human health, then building the tools to navigate it."

03 / The Framework

Eight charted territories.

D_Ψ / 01
Psychological

Emotional regulation, cognitive patterns, and mental resilience. The base camp for every other expedition.

D_Ph / 02
Physical

Movement, nutrition, sleep, and biological systems. The terrain your body moves through daily.

D_In / 03
Intellectual

Continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and cognitive growth. The frontier of mental expansion.

D_So / 04
Social

Meaningful relationships, community building, and interpersonal dynamics. The human network.

D_Sp / 05
Spiritual

Purpose, meaning, and alignment with personal values. The compass that gives direction to all others.

D_Vo / 06
Vocational

Professional fulfillment, skill development, and career satisfaction. The path you build through work.

D_Fi / 07
Financial

Economic stability, financial literacy, and resource management. The provisions for the journey.

D_En / 08
Environmental

Physical surroundings, ecological health, and living conditions. The landscape that shapes the expedition.
