Who We Are

We are not a wellness company. We are the research institution that wellness should be built on.

Founded in 2023 by Ashley Williams, Founder and Chief of Research, Divinity Science operates where quantum theory meets neuroscience meets behavioral science meets data science.

Our core output is the 8-Dimension Wellness Framework: a measurable, testable model for total human health. Not a trend. Not a philosophy. Science with falsifiable predictions and real-world applications.

"We don't study one piece of health. We study the whole system, and we build the science to prove it works."
The Framework

Eight dimensions. Complete science.

D.Ψ
Psychological
Mental health, emotional regulation, cognitive patterns, neurological resilience.
D.Ph
Physical
Physiological health, movement, nutrition, sleep architecture, biological markers.
D.In
Intellectual
Cognitive growth, learning capacity, creative problem-solving, knowledge systems.
D.So
Social
Interpersonal bonds, community integration, communication, relational health.
D.Sp
Spiritual
Purpose, meaning-making, existential grounding, consciousness exploration.
D.Vo
Vocational
Professional fulfillment, skill development, career alignment, productive contribution.
D.Fi
Financial
Economic stability, resource management, wealth-building behaviors, financial literacy.
D.En
Environmental
Physical surroundings, ecological connection, spatial design, environmental impact.
