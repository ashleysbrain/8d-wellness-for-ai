Who We Are

Research at the edge of science.

Divinity Science sits at the intersection of quantum theory, neuroscience, behavioral science, and data science. We've identified 8 distinct dimensions of human wellness, each with its own science, its own metrics, its own interventions.

This isn't incremental improvement. It's a new framework for understanding what human health actually means, built from first principles and designed to scale.

Founded by Ashley Williams, Founder & Chief of Research

"We're not building another wellness product. We're building the scientific foundation that every wellness product should be built on."
The Framework

Eight dimensions. Fully mapped.

D_Ψ / 01
Psychological

Emotional regulation, cognitive patterns, and mental resilience. The foundation everything else is built on.

D_Ph / 02
Physical

Movement, nutrition, sleep, biological systems. The body's operating parameters, optimized.

D_In / 03
Intellectual

Continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and cognitive expansion.

D_So / 04
Social

Meaningful relationships, community building, interpersonal dynamics.

D_Sp / 05
Spiritual

Purpose, meaning, and alignment with personal values.

D_Vo / 06
Vocational

Professional fulfillment, skill development, career satisfaction.

D_Fi / 07
Financial

Economic stability, financial literacy, and resource management.

D_En / 08
Environmental

Physical surroundings, ecological health, and living conditions.
